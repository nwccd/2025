Camp Challenge - Derby Car Race
Cabins will create a derby car!
Only materials found in the derby kit can be used to build the cars.
Materials
Derby Car Kits will be provided on Sunday evening.
For your Derby car, the Recreational team may provide surprise materials which can be bought with points or won in competition.
What to do?
You will build your car to race against your opposing gender cabin.
We have a derby car track that is over 30 feet long.
When?
Thursday night at beginning of camp celebration.
Make sure you have your car ready before Thursday dinner so you can show your car off! They will be on display in the cafeteria.
No tinkering or adjusting allowed once you turn in your car at dinner on Thursday.
