Point System Rules - Colored Batman
Overall Point System

The cabins of opposite gender will war against one another to get their color on the batman doll. HS girls v HS boys, MS girls v MS boys, ES girls v ES boys.

Each cabin will select their color (options: red, blue, green, yellow).

The goal is to collect opportunities to shoot at the doll with a water gun that contains their color (water dye with color).

Whichever color dominates the doll wins the war between cabins. A prize will be given.

If colors mix together on the doll, then it is considered cancellation of each other. The goal is to get as much of the cabin's color on the doll as possible.

At the end of week after Water Fest, the winning cabin and gender gets to put whip cream on the opposing cabin's staffs' faces.

Points

Popsicle sticks of different colors

How to collect points to buy opportunities to color the batman

Win nightly challenge versus opposing gender cabin

Memory verses (entire cabin must recite the verse to a DPT member)

Cabins. Will be observed daily starting Monday and ending on Thursday. Most clean and most creative design will be awarded. Messy cabins will be penalized by losing points.

Games and activities throughout the day will have opportunities for cabins to collect rewards.

Fishing: if you catch fishes then you earn points.

Setting up the tables and clean-up for meal times will award rewards. Please note that your cabin should be on time and do quality work with a good attitude.

Collecting the most water balloon debris will award rewards.

Good sportsmanship acts and kind acts will also be awarded.

Poor sportsmanship acts and being late will result in a penalty.

Elementary campers will have opportunities to collect rewards during class time.

Winning

Elem girls v Elem boys, MS girls v MS boys, HS girls v HS boys. Altogether: Boys v Girls

There will be one cabin winner in each age group and the reward will be given after Water Fest.

Prize - whip cream to the face of opposing gender cabin staff.

What can you use points for?

1. Buy lead weight for 3 points.

2. Cut cars for 10 points.

3. Buy lubricant for 10 points.

4. Buy squirting colors on the Batman doll for 10 points.
