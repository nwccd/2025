How to collect points

Win nightly challenge versus opposing gender cabin

Memory verses (entire cabin must recite the verse to a Prayer Ministry Team member)

Cabins. Will be observed daily starting Monday and ending on Thursday. Most clean and most creative design will be awarded. Messy cabins will be penalized by losing points.

Games and activities throughout the day will have opportunities for cabins to collect rewards.

Fishing: if you catch fishes then you earn points.

Setting up the tables and clean-up for meal times will award rewards. Please note that your cabin should be on time and do quality work with a good attitude.

Collecting the most water balloon debris will award rewards.

Good sportsmanship acts and kind acts will also be awarded.

Poor sportsmanship acts and being late will result in a penalty.

Elementary campers will have opportunities to collect rewards during class time.

Winning

Elem girls v Elem boys, MS girls v MS boys, HS girls v HS boys. Altogether: Boys v Girls

There will be one cabin winner in each age group and the reward will be given after Water Fest.

Prize - whip cream to the face of opposing gender cabin staff.
